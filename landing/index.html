FAQ

Anything else?

Can I use it without knowing an instrument?

+
Yes — that's the whole point. Drums are two-thumb pads, and the melody and bass lanes only hold notes that fit the key, so anything you tap sounds right. No notation or theory needed.

How does humming become a track?

+
On the melody screen, tap the mic and hum. We analyse pitch and timing and place it on the track as tidy, in-key MIDI — fully editable, just like notes you tapped in by hand.

Is there a watermark on free-tier songs?

+
No. Free users get every creating and editing feature with no watermark or quality cap. Pro unlocks export and cloud sync only.

Can I cancel anytime?

+
Yes — cancel anytime in the App Store / Google Play. Pro features remain active until the end of your billing cycle.

Which devices are supported?

+
iOS 15+ and Android 10+, optimised for landscape. The web editor is coming soon and will be included for Pro subscribers.

Who owns the songs I make?

+
You do — and commercial use is completely free.
