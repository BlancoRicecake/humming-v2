Introducing HumTrack 🎧

A tap-first mobile DAW for making beats and loops on the go.

- Four-track workflow: melody / bass / drums / vocal
- Two input modes — Pads for two-thumb play, Grid for precision
- Chord mode — one pad, one key-based triad
- 7-day free trial, then go Pro for unlimited songs + MIDI / audio export
- Sign in with Apple, Google, or email
