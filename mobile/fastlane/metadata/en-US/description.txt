Can't read sheet music? Don't know chords? Just hum — HumTrack turns the melody in your head into a full multitrack song in under a minute.

HumTrack analyzes your vocal recording with AI and turns it into piano, bass, drums, and a vocal track you can edit together. Idea-to-mix without writing a single note by hand.

■ Hum → Multitrack
Hum, whistle, or sing — pitch and timing are extracted automatically. Chord expansion, bass lines, and drum patterns are added in one pass.

■ Auto Key Detection + Pitch Assistant
Even if your pitch wobbles a little, HumTrack detects the song's key (C, Am, G, etc.) and gently nudges notes to fit. Keep your phrasing — lose the bum notes.

■ Quantize (Multi-Track Sync)
If tracks drift apart in time, quantize locks them onto the same grid. Choose how strict — preserve human feel or align perfectly.

■ CapCut-Style Timeline Editor
Slice, trim, magnetic snap, zoom, solo/mute, track loop. Familiar gestures from video editors.

■ On-Device SoundFont Playback
SF2 instrument library bundled in the app. Preview and full mix playback work offline.

■ MIDI / WAV Export (Pro)
Export to standard MIDI and WAV — open in Logic, Ableton, GarageBand, FL Studio. Detected chords, key, and timing are preserved.

■ Cloud Sync (Pro)
Pick up the same project on another device. 5 GB safe cloud storage. Your files stay even after subscription ends.
