HumTrack is the easiest way to turn your humming into a real song. Sing or hum a melody — get MIDI, chords, and a full multi-track session in seconds. No music theory, no learning curve.

A mobile DAW and music production app built for beginners, songwriters, beat makers, and anyone who can hum a tune.

[ Features ]
- Hum, sing, or play → auto pitch, rhythm, and chord detection
- Multi-track editor: vocal + piano + bass + drums + synth
- Metronome · Quantize (beat correction) · Auto key detection
- Export to MIDI · WAV
- Local save — start instantly, no login required
